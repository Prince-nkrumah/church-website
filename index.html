About Our Church

We are a welcoming community dedicated to spreading the love of Christ. Our mission is to nurture spiritual growth, serve our neighbors, and create a family of believers who support one another in faith and life.

Founded in 2010, we've grown from a small Bible study group to a vibrant congregation with diverse ministries for all ages and backgrounds.
